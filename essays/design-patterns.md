## Design Patterns

Being able to present important life lessons to children at an early stage creates a type of process that allows children to focus on the design thinking process. Design patterns are everywhere looking at something in a creative way such as object programming and learning the structural compositions and behaviors that a program does. 

Creative patterns could be a Calendar used in Java. A structural pattern in coding could be a coffee shop menu and a customer may want a coffee with cream, milk, and sugar. You can use subclasses to describe the drink, what type it is, and the add-ons. A coder must be object-oriented. They must quickly adapt to each environment they face whether it's a different programming language or software. A behavioral pattern would be how classes interact with each other like a restaurant. The order is the command that is invoked by the waiter and is handed over to the receiver (chef).
